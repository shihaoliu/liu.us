My Next Chapter- CMU Master's in Machine Science

Created in June 11, 2025

2025   ·   news

I’m excited to share that I’ll be starting a new chapter this fall as a Master in Machine Science student at Carnegie Mellon University.

I will be conducting research with Yueqi Song under the advisement of Professor Graham Neubig. I’m particularly looking forward to contributing to their work and delving deeper into the world of machine learning and natural language processing.

This is a fantastic opportunity, and I can’t wait to see what I’ll learn and accomplish. More updates to come!
